Issuing Certificates and Statements Service

This is an electronic service that allows parents and students of government schools and private schools that only follow ministry standards to extract and print the following:

  • Grades report (mid-first semester - end of the first semester - mid-second semester)
  • End of the current academic year certificate
  • Certificates of previous years starting from the academic year (2011-2012)
  • To whom it may concern certificate (passing / failing) for previous years starting from the academic year (2011-2012)
  • To whom it may concern certificate (registration) for the current year
  • To whom it may concern certificate (registration) for previous years starting from the academic year (2011-2012)
